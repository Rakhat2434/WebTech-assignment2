This is my midterm project: Equipment Management System.

This project is an Equipment Management System designed for small to medium-sized businesses and IT departments. It will simplify inventory management, maintenance scheduling, and reporting, helping administrators efficiently monitor and manage all equipment from a single, intuitive interface.
